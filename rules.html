Online Flea Market

Website Rules

  1. All items uploaded must be legal and appropriate.
  2. No fake or misleading listings.
  3. Buyers and sellers must communicate respectfully.
  4. The platform is not responsible for any transaction disputes.
  5. Items that violate rules may be removed.

Platform Guidelines

For Buyers

  1. Browse through products
  2. When interested products are encountered, click 'Product Details' to view more
  3. Please contact the seller through the phone number attatched in Product Details for purchase or rental

For Sellers

  1. Click the 'Upload' button to upload items to be sold
  2. Sellers must provide accurate item and seller information.
  3. Once item is sold or rented out, sellers should delete the product from the homepage to prevent confusion.
